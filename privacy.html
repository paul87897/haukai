PRIVACY STATEMENT

We collect personal information from you, including your name, email and phone number.

We collect your personal information in order to make and alter reservations for patrons of the restaurant. If you choose not to provide your name and contact information we'll be unable to make a reservation for you. You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at admin@haukai.co.nz.
